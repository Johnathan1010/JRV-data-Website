About Us Services

Executive Summary

AI Health is an intelligent application that guides users to sustainable weight loss and improved wellness by calculating a personalized calorie deficit and offering clean, organic meal plans. The system mimics deductive medical logic to prioritize food and lifestyle over medication.

Problem Statement

Obesity, diabetes, and heart disease are often linked to excessive intake of processed foods and poor dietary habits. Despite available information, people often struggle to follow a personalized, sustainable plan that leads to real change.

Vision

AI Health delivers daily calorie targets, symptom-driven suggestions, and meal plans rich in whole foods. The app empowers individuals with proactive health advice that emphasizes prevention and sustainable habits over reliance on prescriptions.

System Design Overview

Calorie Deficit Calculator: Determines BMR and TDEE to suggest safe daily caloric deficits.
Meal Planning Engine: Prioritizes organic vegetables, lean meats, and whole foods while avoiding processed ingredients.
Symptom-Based Guidance: Helps users adjust lifestyle choices based on fatigue, bloating, and other inputs using logical recommendations.

  • Snack: Almonds + Blueberries

Technology Stack

Frontend: React Native, Next.js
Backend: Python, FastAPI
AI Layer: GPT-based NLP engine
Database: PostgreSQL
Integrations: USDA FoodData Central, Fitbit, Apple Health

Competitive Advantage

AI Health differentiates itself by focusing on organic-first meal planning, symptom-based deduction, and natural solutions. Unlike mainstream calorie apps, AI Health guides users toward healthier food and lifestyle decisions while minimizing medication dependence.

Business Model

Freemium: Basic calorie tracking and symptom logging.
Premium: $9.99/month for custom plans, AI-driven insights, wearable sync.
B2B: Corporate wellness packages for employers and insurers.

Future Enhancements

Integration with genetic data, gut biome analysis, and telehealth consultations. Visual food recognition and real-time calorie estimation also planned.
